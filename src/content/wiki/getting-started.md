# Getting Started with Hack Club

Welcome! If you're brand new to Hack Club, this is the right place. Here's how to get plugged in.

## Step 1: Join the Slack

The Hack Club [[Slack]] is where most of the community hangs out. It has over 50,000 members and hundreds of channels for every interest.

1. Go to [hackclub.com/slack](https://hackclub.com/slack)
2. Sign up with your school email
3. Introduce yourself in `#introduce-yourself`

## Step 2: Find or Start a Club

Check if there's already a [[Clubs|club at your school]]. If not, you can [[Starting a Club|start one]] — it's free and Hack Club will support you.

## Step 3: Build Something

The best way to learn is by building. Check out:

- [workshops.hackclub.com](https://workshops.hackclub.com) — guided projects
- [[Sprig]] — make a game and get a free console
- [[Blot]] — build a drawing machine

## Step 4: Share What You Build

Every Hack Clubber is encouraged to [[Ship]] their work. Post it to [[Scrapbook]], our daily update feed.

## What's Hack Club About?

Hack Club is **not** a class, a bootcamp, or a competition. It's a community of people who love making things. The vibe is:

> Make something. Share it. Help others make things too.
